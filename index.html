Our Mission

Hope Lighter's mission is to give high school students from all parts of the world a chance to lead and to help some of the best non-profit organizations out there. Our mission to help non-profits and high school students is achieved by the use of networking, which will lead to more high schools and students helping us to achieve this mission.
